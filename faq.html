Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your questions about investing with Beryl Group Estate.

Are your lands genuinely titled?

Yes. Each estate has verifiable documentation. We encourage independent verification by your lawyer or surveyor before you make any payment.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes. We offer flexible payment plans depending on the estate and plot size. You can spread payments over 3, 6, or 12 months.

Do you allow site inspections?

Absolutely. Inspections are free but must be booked ahead. We can arrange for a physical inspection or a virtual tour if you are not available.

When do I get allocation?

Allocation is based on your payment plan and stated terms for each project. Typically, physical allocation is done after full payment and documentation.

Can I buy from outside Nigeria?

Yes. We support diaspora buyers with virtual inspections and secure payment routes. We have many clients abroad who have successfully invested with us.
